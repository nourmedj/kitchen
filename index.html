Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a project?

From design approval to final installation, our typical timeline is 8-12 weeks. Custom materials may extend this.

Do you handle plumbing and electrical?

Yes. We offer a "Turnkey" service where we manage all tradespeople, or we can work alongside your chosen contractors.

What is the starting budget for a Maison kitchen?

Our bespoke projects typically start at $35,000, including design, fabrication, and installation.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, all our cabinetry comes with a 10-year craftsmanship warranty, and hardware carries a lifetime guarantee.
